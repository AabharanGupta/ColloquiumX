RECKON

11 February 24
Location

During this event, participants will be challenged to solve cybersecurity exercises on an ISTE platform, discovering hidden flags in vulnerable programs and websites with clues and hints provided for each question. Team needs a minimum of 3 players to register for the event.Teams solve questions using different deciphering techniques, earning points for each correct answer. Points decrease as more teams solve questions, adding a dynamic challenge. The event emphasizes speed and accuracy. The first team to clear the final level is declared the winner.

FALLACY

12 February 24
LOCATION

Fallacy is an engaging event centered around coding, debugging, and collaborative problem solving. The event features two rounds: "Blind Coding, " where participants tackle problems with monitors off, relying on coding skills and mental visualization, and "Debug the Code, " where teams rapidly correct errors in a relay format. The team with the most points at the end of both rounds will be declared the winner.

CIRCUITRIX

13 February 24
Location

"Circuitrix" is an exciting electronic circuit-building competition highlighting knowledge, teamwork, and hands-on skills across two rounds. In Round 1, "Knowledge Blitz, " teams engage in a buzzer showdown, strategically using virtual currency to answer electronic riddles. In the "Circuit Forging" round, teams construct functional circuits for given problem statements, incorporating essential components and purchasable upgrades. The winning team, evaluated on complexity, innovation, and accuracy, presents the best fully functional circuit

BUZZINGA

15 February 24
Location

Buzzinga, inspired by the popular shows "Jeopardy" and "Catch 21, " tests participants' skills in three exciting rounds. In the first round, participants choose a Trivia genre, bet, and earn currency for correct answers. The second round is a buzzer round with questions of assigned values. The event concludes with the "Catch 21" finale, challenging finalists to swiftly answer trivia questions to accumulate cards and reach a total of 21 for victory.
